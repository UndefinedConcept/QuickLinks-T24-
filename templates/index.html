Air Quality Information

Air quality refers to the condition of the air within our surroundings. It is determined by the amount of pollutants present in the air.

Key Pollutants

  • Particulate Matter (PM2.5 and PM10)
  • Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2)
  • Sulfur Dioxide (SO2)
  • Carbon Monoxide (CO)
  • Ozone (O3)

Why is Air Quality Important?

Poor air quality can lead to health problems, environmental damage, and economic costs. Monitoring air quality helps to protect public health and the environment.
